## Guidelines for Dictation:

**Good things to mention:**
- Industry or type of company
- The specific problem/pain point
- What systems were involved (SAP, Excel, Workday, Ariba, etc.)
- What you did to solve it
- Quantifiable results (time saved, cost reduced, errors eliminated, etc.)
- Any unique challenges or innovations
